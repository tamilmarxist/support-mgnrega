ЁЯУЛ Key Concerns

We reject VB-GRAMG Act! It denies the legal right to work guaranteed to all rural families seeking employment under MGNREG Act.

тЪая╕П Critical Issues

  • Demand-Driven to Supply-Driven: VB-GRAMG changes the fundamental principle from demand-driven funding to a centralized supply-driven system with predetermined budgets.
  • Budget Limitations: Once budget funds are exhausted, workers' legal right to work will be denied.
  • State Burden: States must bear 40% of total costs, severely impacting poorer states and reducing job opportunities in high-need areas.
  • Agricultural Season Restrictions: Mandatory 60-day halt during peak agricultural periods forces landless agricultural workers into low-wage private employment.
  • Wage Floor Removal: The year-round "wage floor" protection provided by MGNREGA is eliminated, attacking workers' legal rights and bargaining power.
  • Centralization: Powers are concentrated in the hands of the central government, violating federal principles.
  • Removal of Mahatma Gandhi's Name: This disrespects the legacy of the freedom struggle and the historic MGNREGA Act of 2005.

тЬЕ Our Demands

  • Increase work days to 200 days
  • Set minimum wage at тВ╣700 per day
  • Ensure daily or weekly wage payments without delay
  • Grant village assembly authority for monitoring and planning
  • Integrate MGNREGA with agriculture and allied sectors
  • End agricultural crisis, rural debt, forced migration, and farmer suicides
